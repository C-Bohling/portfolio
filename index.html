Caleb Bohling

My name is Caleb and I am a highschool homeschooler who loves coding. I started coding when I was 10 with scratch (I guess that counts). After I learned the limits of scratch I decided to learn something new. In 2018 I started learning Python 3 with some simple learn-to-code books. I was only 12 then and did not try to learn things like lists or objects. Because of that cap I was setting for myself I really did not do to many interesting things with python until 2020. At that point I learned lists, dictionaries, and objects. Whole new sets of possibilities opened up and I started building much more fun projects like simple data analyzers and programs to help me with my latin practice.

In 2022 I was hired to build a job tracking website for a commercial furniture company, so I learned Django. I don't think I was ever very good at Django, but I successfully built a fairly simple job tracking website. It was a fairly limited site and I had not really learned how to use JavaScript, so there was much left to be improved. I am now taking Codecademy's full-stack development course, and I am learning so much. I am excited to see what happens next!
